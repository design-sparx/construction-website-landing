about us

DEBCO LIMITED COMPANY was Registered in the year 2023 under Companies Act 2015 as a Private Limited Company. DLC is a multi-disciplinary DESIGN AND BUILD CONSORTIUM hub which comprises of various construction professionals of Architects, Project Managers, Quantity Surveyors (QS), Mechanical Engineers, Electrical Engineers and Structural Engineers who at each in their various capacities and departments add their expertise and experience to the overall functioning and operations of the larger company.

Since Inception, the firm has been providing Design Consultancy, Building Construction and Civil Engineering works to both Private Companies and Government Institutions.

We are versatile company with a reputation for outstanding quality workmanship, which has broadened our focus with a diversified new fields of specialisation and modern technologies .

Through our highly skilled and competent staff, we endeavor to deliver to our client’s high quality workmanship within the stipulated time period.
